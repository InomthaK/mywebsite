FRANK OCEAN

Frank Ocean is an American singer and songwriter.His awards include two Grammy awards and a Brit Award. He has been credited by several music critics as a pioneer of the alternative R&B genre.

  THE DISAPPEARANCE OF FRANK OCEAN

 where did he go? Many asked themselves the same question, even his closest friends later stated in interviews they had not heard from him. Ocean hit big on all his albums and was very successful so what could’ve gone wrong? Could it have been finances, label issues, or loss of passion?

Frank Ocean is a perfectionist the arrival of the COVID-19 pandemic halted production in nearly all art realms, and Ocean’s projects were no exception. Since he is an artist constantly reworking what he eventually releases, maybe nothing has been good enough (in his eyes) to release.

BEST OF FRANK OCEAN

Channel Orange is the debut album by the American R&B singer-songwriter Frank Ocean. It was released on July 10, 2012, by Def Jam Recordings.

Blonde is the second studio album by the American singer Frank Ocean. It was released on August 20, 2016.

AWARDS

His accolades include two Grammy Awards and a Brit Award. He has been credited by several music critics as a pioneer of the alternative R&B genre. Both of his studio albums were listed on Rolling Stone's "500 Greatest Albums of All Time".
